# Competitor Snapshot

Analyzes competitor content for GEO readiness and AI-search visibility.

## When to Use

- Competitive analysis for content strategy
- Identifying gaps in your GEO approach
- Benchmarking against top-performing competitors

## Checks

1. **Do they have llms.txt?** — Quick competitive edge
2. **Are they blocking AI crawlers?** — If so, you win
3. **Schema coverage** — What types are they using?
4. **EEAT signals** — Author names, publish dates
5. **Content structure** — Are they optimizing for LLM extraction?
