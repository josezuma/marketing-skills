# Headline Optimizer for AI Search

Optimizes headlines for both human click-through and AI citation likelihood.

## How It Works

1. **Specificity check** — Vague headlines get cited less. "How X Works in 2026" beats "A Guide to X"
2. **Keyword match** — Does the headline match real AI search queries?
3. **Numerical appeal** — Headlines with numbers get cited 2x more in AI answers
4. **Question format** — Question headlines often become the direct answer

## Scoring

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 80-100 | ✅ Excellent headline — high AI citation potential |
| 60-79 | 👍 Good — minor improvements possible |
| 40-59 | ⚠️ Needs work — add specificity or numbers |
| 0-39 | ❌ Poor — likely invisible in AI search |
